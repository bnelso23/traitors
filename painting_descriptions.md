# The Painting Challenge - Artwork Pose Booklet

This document compiles highly specific, cheeky pose and composition descriptions for the 46 classical paintings in the gallery. These descriptions serve as the prompt booklet for the **"Painting Challenge"** tableau vivant game, where contestants must arrange themselves to act out these historic scenes.

---

## 1. Walfang Im Polarmeer
* **Artist**: Abraham Storck
* **File Name**: `abraham_storck_-_walfang_im_polarmeer.jpg`
* **Local File Path**: `paintings/Abraham Storck - Walfang im Polarmeer - (MeisterDrucke-677091).jpg`
* **Pose Description**: 
  A frosty rowboat packed tighter than a commuter train. Five cold souls desperately pretend to row while one at the bow stands and dramatically readies a harpoon. One poor soul must lay on the floor acting as a thrashing whale, trying not to get accidentally poked. In the background, a line of actors pretend to adjust sails on a ship deck.
